AI Search Checklist

8 Steps to Get Found by Generative AIs

Marcos Luciano · AI Lead & AI Search Specialist @ V4 Company

☐ Step 1: Extractable Headings

Are your headings (H2, H3) clear questions or topics that an AI can extract as standalone answers?

  • H2 describe the main topic of each section
  • H3 are specific questions or subtopics
  • Each heading works as an independent answer

☐ Step 2: Optimized Paragraphs

Do your paragraphs have 2-4 sentences and work as standalone answers for RAG extraction?

  • Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences each)
  • First sentence directly answers the question
  • Following sentences provide context

☐ Step 3: Lists and Definitions

Did you include lists and explicit definitions that AIs love to cite?

  • Ordered and unordered lists for structured info
  • Definitions in "X is Y that does Z" format
  • Technical terms defined within the sentence

☐ Step 4: FAQ Schema Implemented

Did you implement JSON-LD with FAQ Schema in the article?

  • FAQ Schema with complete questions and answers
  • Real questions from your audience (AnswerThePublic, Reddit, PAA)
  • Complete answers (not just "see more")

☐ Step 5: Updated Content

Does your content have a recent update date (last 6 months)?

  • Last modified date visible in the article
  • Content reviewed within the last 6 months
  • References to current data and events

☐ Step 6: Primary Sources Cited

Do you cite primary sources and verifiable data?

  • Studies, papers, and official data cited
  • Links to original sources
  • Numerical data with clear attribution

☐ Step 7: Multi-Platform Presence

Is your brand present on multiple platforms that AIs index?

  • LinkedIn with complete profile and activity
  • Medium, Substack, or Hashnode with articles
  • GitHub with relevant projects
  • Wikipedia (if applicable)

☐ Step 8: Active Monitoring

Do you regularly monitor your presence in AI responses?

  • Test in ChatGPT: does your brand appear?
  • Test in Perplexity: is your brand cited?
  • Test in Google AI Overviews: is your brand there?
  • Metric: Share of AI Voice vs competitors
