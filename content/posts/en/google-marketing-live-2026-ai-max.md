Google Marketing Live 2026 may be remembered as the day traditional search died. Google announced AI Max, a new campaign format that inserts ads contextually within AI Overviews — not beside them, but integrated into the generated text.

## AI Max: How It Works

Unlike traditional Shopping Ads and Search Ads, AI Max uses Gemini 3.5 itself to understand user intent and suggest products or services as a natural part of the generated response. The format includes:

- **Product cards** displayed within the AI Overview text
- **Sponsored citations** with links in the response body
- **Direct actions** such as scheduling and purchasing without leaving the Overview

Initial beta data shows CTR 3.2x higher than traditional Search Ads, but with 45% higher CPC — suggesting the format delivers better conversion but at a premium cost.

## The End of the Traditional Auction

Google's auction model has changed. Ad ranking now considers not only max CPC and Quality Score but also a "Context Score" — how relevant the product is to the specific generative query. Advertisers that do not optimize for conversational intent will simply disappear from results.

## What This Means

For advertisers, the message is urgent: those who still think of SEO and Search Ads as separate channels are making a strategic mistake. The convergence of organic search, paid search, and generative response requires a unified "AI Presence" strategy — being visible in AI Overviews both organically and through paid media. Brands that do not start structuring data semantically and optimizing for LLM extraction will lose 60% of search traffic by the end of 2027.
